# ZeroPay Mobile UX Architecture & Screen List

## 1. Customer Flow
- **Onboarding & Entry:** Splash Screen, Landing Screen, Benefits Carousel, Escrow/Trust Education, Wallet Intro.
- **Authentication:** Phone Auth, OTP Verification, Role Selection (Customer/Merchant).
- **Core Experience:** Customer Dashboard (Overview), Wallet Center (Assets/Portfolio), Marketplace Discovery (Search, Categories).
- **Commerce:** Merchant Profiles & Details, Checkout Flow, QR Payments, Escrow Creation & Tracking, Milestone Progress.
- **Support & Trust:** AI Negotiation (Chat Commerce), Dispute Submission, Evidence Upload, Court Participation, Trust Center, Activity Timeline.
- **Management:** Notifications, Profile, Settings, Security Center, Support.

## 2. Merchant Flow
- **Business Setup:** Merchant Onboarding, Verification, Store Setup, Profile Management.
- **Operations:** Merchant Dashboard (Revenue/Escrow Analytics), Storefront & Order Management, Milestone Management.
- **Financials:** Payout Center, Settlement Tracking, Reputation Center (Reviews).
- **Resolution:** Dispute Queue, Court Management.
- **Developer Tools:** Webhook Center, Developer Mode (API Keys, Logs).

## 3. Hybrid & State Management
- **Switching:** Role/Workspace Switching (Customer <-> Merchant).
- **Escrow States:** Draft, Pending, Created, Locked, Active, Milestone Release, Dispute, Resolved, Completed.
- **AI Copilot:** Risk Assessment, Fair Pricing Assistant, Dispute Summaries, Contract Explanation.
- **Arbitration Court:** Case Dashboard, Evidence Viewer, Juror Board, Voting/Consensus.

## 4. Wallet & Infrastructure
- **Assets:** Multi-Chain Portfolio (Cardano, USDC), Transaction History, Send/Receive, Address Book.
- **System:** Network Status, Ledger Explorer, Audit Logs, Telemetry Center.

## 5. UI & Motion System
- **Components:** Typography, Color Tokens (Indigo/Violet), Cards, Buttons, Bottom Sheets, Trust Badges, Progress Steppers.
- **Motion:** Page Transitions, Card Expansions, Role Switching Animations, AI Interaction States.
